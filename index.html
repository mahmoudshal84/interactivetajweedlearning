About Tajweed Learning

This interactive website is designed to help you learn the rules of Tajweed for proper Quranic recitation. It serves as a supplement for learning, aiding teachers with instruction and supporting students with individual study and review, this website is NOT meant to replace a qualified teacher.

This website covers only selected key information from the most important topics in the book "Summary of Al-Muneer Book for Tajweed Rules". There is far more to the science of Tajweed than what this website covers.
