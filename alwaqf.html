Obligatory Stops (الوقف اللازم)

Complete Meaning

Stop required to prevent misunderstanding

[stop] وَمَا يَعْلَمُ تَأْوِيلَهُ ۗ إِلَّا اللَّهُ

Prevent Contradiction

Stop to avoid contradicting meaning

[stop] لَا رَيْبَ ۛ فِيهِ

Prohibited Stops (الوقف الممنوع)

Incomplete Meaning

Middle of connected phrase

اللَّهِ [stop] بِسْمِ

Distorted Meaning

Changes intended meaning

الصَّلَاةَ [stop] لَا تَقْرَبُو

Within Word

Stopping mid-word

مَانِ[stop] الرَّحْ

Permitted Stops (الوقف الجائز)

Complete Stop (تام)

End of complete meaning

أُولَٰئِكَ هُمُ الْمُفْلِحُونَ ۚ

Good Stop (كافي)

Meaning complete but connected

الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ ۝

Fair Stop (حسن)

Meaning understood but incomplete

الرَّحْمَٰنِ ۝
